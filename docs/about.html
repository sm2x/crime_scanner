Crime Scanner

About

Crime Scanner allows you to interactively visualise street level crime and anti-social behaviour (ASB) incidents in your area. You can filter counts of crime and ASB over the last three years and see the results in linked visualisations.

Data

The application retrieves police recorded crime and incidents of anti-social behaviour in England from data.police.uk. Thirteen different crime categories are available. Crime and anti-social behaviour incidents can be filtered by Local Authority District, Electoral ward and best-fitting Lower-layer Super Output Area (LSOA) levels.

Please note:

  • The location of crimes and incidents of anti-social behaviour are approximate. Crime and ASB data published on data.police.uk are subject to a location anonymisation process which is intended to ensure the privacy of victims of crime. A review of the process can be found in Tompson et al. (2015).
  • Some offences such as domestic violence are not available.
  • Not all crimes are reported to the police.
  • Crime rates have been calculated using the number of residents in each area. This is an imperfect denominator because it doesn't necessarily reflect the daytime population of an area or the number of criminal targets available.
